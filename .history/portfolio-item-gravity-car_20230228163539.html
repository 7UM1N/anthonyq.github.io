Gravity Car

a makeshift vehicle which operates solely on gravitational energy

S C R O L L

What?

• Vehicle which can operate solely on the gravitational energy of a 1kg mass hanging 60 cm in the air.
• Won competition for design of fastest gravity car.

How?

• Fabricated initial prototype using PEX pipe, Nerf gun parts, and duct tape to gauge design feasibility.
• Designed scaled 3D model to prepare team for construction of the design.
• 3D printed unique parts with ABS and machined high stress parts using steel.

Results

• Able to consistently launch its projectile above 1.5m when angled vertically.
• Can adjust its launch angle in any direction within 20 degrees from the vertical (upwards).
• Wide base prevents tipping during use.
• An aesthetic design achieved through many CAD revisions and spray painting.

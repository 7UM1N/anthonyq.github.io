Impeller

centrifugal pump for robotic suction arm

S C R O L L

What?

• An centrifugal-pump and enclosure designed to generate vacuum force for a suction feature on a robotic Lego arm.

How?

• Designed 3D model of centrifugal-pump in SolidWorks.
• Ran flow simulations in Solidworks to determine the most optimal shape for the centrifugal-pump.
• 3D printed centrifugal-pump as well as enclosure for centrifugal-pump using PLA filament.

Results

• Generates suction with the use of only one Lego EV3 motor and several gear ratios.
• Can generate enough suction to lift a 4 g object, demonstrated by lifting a napkin.

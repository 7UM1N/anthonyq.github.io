Launcho

spring powered toy rocket

S C R O L L

What?

Launcho is an innovative toy, created by a team of 5 people including me. It is a spring powered rocket launcher that can fire on command while being able to adjust its angle of fire in all 3 dimensions.

How?

“Looks Like” Model

I was in charge of creating a visual representation of the product. The objective of this was to get a more concrete idea of our end goal. The model was created in Solidworks with each individual component being modeled then added to the assembly. After this model was created, we then went off to brainstorm materials and exact dimensions each individual part.

Exact Model

Knowing all the dimensions I teamed up with another group member to create the final model which was then used to 3D print several parts

“Machining

Some parts had to be stronger than 3D printing allowed for. Thus, we opted for other materials such as steel and PEX. However, these had to be machined to the correct dimensions. Above is a picture of the machined PEX pipe.

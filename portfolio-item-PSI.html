Press Sinter Infiltrate (PSI) Process Animation

Streamlining Onboarding and Enhancing Education: A Visual Journey with Electrical Contacts Limited

S C R O L L

The Objective: Streamlining Onboarding and Enhancing Education

Streamlining Onboarding: When new employees join a company, the initial days can often be overwhelming. Getting acclimated to the processes, culture, and expectations can be challenging. As an ECL Intern, I aimed to simplify this process, making it smoother and more efficient for their newest team members.

Enhance Education: Understanding the intricacies of the PSI process is crucial for ECL employees. To achieve this, I wanted to go beyond traditional training methods and provide a visual representation that would engage and educate simultaneously.

Bringing the Vision to Life

To turn this vision into reality, I leveraged a combination of technology and research:

1. SolidWorks Modeling: I modeled and assembled assets for the animation using SolidWorks. This included essential components like a furnace and powder press, which are integral to the PSI process.

2. SolidWorks Motion Study: Animating these components required precision and expertise. SolidWorks' motion study feature allowed me to bring their assets to life by adjusting mating properties, creating a dynamic and accurate representation of the PSI process.

3. In-Depth Research: To ensure the animation was not just visually appealing but also educationally valuable, I conducted extensive research on the PSI process. This research laid the foundation for an accurate and detailed script, making the animation a powerful educational tool.
